HOBBIES

fly fishing

My go-to hobby is fly fishing. Nothing makes me happier than being on the flats with small waves crashing peacefully and having my fly rod in hand. I enjoy guiding, deckhand and skipper work.

Hand crafting

I take interest in wood work and I build my own split cane fly rods. I enjoy welding and machining/fabricating precision parts.

Film making

On any adventure I make good efforts to record my experience. I make amateur films for the Zululand Yacht Club based on sailing and community development.

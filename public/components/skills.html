Heavy equipment safety and workmanship

I grew up in an industrial workshop where heavy equipment is used for every task. This includes Forklifts, Loaders/dozers, excavators, cranes and trucks. With the years of experience working around, on and with these machines and equipment I have grown a second nature to always ensure that safety requirements are followed and work is executed in such a manner to prevent future incidents.

Mechanical workmanship

I grew up in an automotive workshop. In the shop, I have been working on suspensions, brake/fuel/cooling systems, engines, drive trains, wiring and brackets for as long as I can remember. Our shop is recommended as one of the go-to shops for wiring ECUs on vehicles (higher-end ECUs like: Haltech, Fueltech, Volvo and Mercedes trucks).

Welding
